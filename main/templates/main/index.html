Project overview

Our project “EarthSaver AI” relates to the environment track and is dedicated to the study of climate change using artificial intelligence and machine learning methods. Our goal is to develop AI solution that supports environmental sustainability and addresses climate change challenges. The topic of our research is determined to Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) provided by United Nations (UN) and adopted by all UN Member States in 2015. Referring to SDG 13 climate change is a real and undeniable threat to our entire civilization.


Policymakers, planners, investors and vulnerable communities need information about future climate so that they can prepare for expected trends and changes. The main purpose of our idea is to develop an AI model to predict temperature changes in different regions of the Earth, trace dependencies and select machine learning models for different climate zones to achieve the highest efficiency of the model. The model is supposed to be trained on datasets with a Creative Commons license.

Our team

EcoMind Innovators is a team from Khmelnytskyi National University (Khmelnytskyi city, Ukraine).

We are students who are not indifferent to the current problems of our planet. Driven by the goal of change towards a better future for humanity, we decided to join GRAILS. Our team consists of four members and two faculty advisors.
